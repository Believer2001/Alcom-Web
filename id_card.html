Alumni ID Card

Alcom has initiated the provision of unique ID cards to all Alumni. ALCOM ID Card was launched on the Alumni day 25th Dec 2014. Since then, ID cards are provided to golden and silver batches and also for convocation batch and also those who request through Web Portal every year. It helps them entering in the college any time & accessing college facilities like computer centre, library, hostel, mess, canteen, guest house, departments. In 2021 total 1,336 ID-cards were provided to alumni. It provides an identification on alumni-day and all other college events. It serves as a memorable token for them.
