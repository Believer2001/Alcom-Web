Guest Lecture

Guest lectures are organized where our notable alumni share their knowledge with budding engineers and ignite their minds for the purpose of social welfare and provide an important educational experience for students based on their real-world life experiences. We organize sessions where our alumni speak about the crucial matters that students need to consider.
With the aim of bridging the gap between speculations and applications, experience and immaturity, to grasp the wisdom and intimate knowledge, the Guest Lectures of Distinguish Alumni organized by Alumni Committee (ALCOM) is a step forward to enlighten young engineers of MNIT.

AboutAlumni Affairs

The purpose of the Deaneries Alumni Affairs is to encourage and strengthens the bond between the Institute and its alumni. The Deaneries urges alumni to give back to the Institute that launched their careers, by supporting the institute in its journey to be a truly world-class institution for higher education and organizes various events with the help of the MNIT Student body that is the Alumni committee (ALCOM) which conducts various activities like organizing guest lectures, Alumni Day and helps in providing an identity card to all alumni.
