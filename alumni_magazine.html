Alcom magazine team, a team full of enthusiasts, passion and creativity, defines moments, gives words to flashes and takes a dunk at stories. Our magazine AlumniTimes connects our acclaimed alumni to students. We share their stories and experiences through the magazine. We share the stories happened throughout the year. A picture can capture a scene but we remove the deceptions of it. Our team focuses on writing about activities related to fests, guest lectures, events, etc. Alumni magazine started in 2013, and since its starting, it has been delivering a great coordination and pace to the committee. There have been 8 publications of the magazine. Pacing forward with time the team has accomplished many heights.
Our objectives are quite simple but important. We bridge the gap between alumni and students. We compile and format the documentation of alumni committee. Magazines are a great way to communicate the richness of events. We provide the information and achievements of the institute and committee to the readers. Our team compiles the data for MNIT, MNITJAA, Alumni committee. Alumni are the roots of any institute. The tree stays upright because of its roots. Alumni can help the students in their career, placements and most importantly life. The magazine keeps them updated and informed. As it is being said, success is at its best when shared.
